Behavioral & STAR Guide
30min/day in Weeks 6–8 · Fix your articulation, not just your stories
🤝
REFERRAL ACTION — Do This April 1, Same Day as Application
You have 3 college contacts at Google with phone numbers. Message all three the same day you apply. A lukewarm referral still moves you from the cold pool (often auto-screened) to a guaranteed recruiter review. You don't need to be close friends. A brief, direct message is enough. Template below.
"Hey [Name], hope you're doing well! I applied to Google SDE-2 today and was wondering if you'd be comfortable referring me. I've been at RingCentral for ~2 years working on [briefly: what you do], and I'm actively prepping. No pressure at all if it's not something you're comfortable with — just thought I'd ask since I respect your work. Thanks either way!"
The STAR Framework
S
Situation
Set the scene. What was the context? Company stage, team size, constraints. Keep this to 2–3 sentences max.
T
Task
What was YOUR specific responsibility? Not the team's — yours. What were you accountable for?
A
Action
What did YOU do? Specific steps. This should be 60% of your answer. Use "I", not "we". Explain your reasoning.
R
Result
What happened? Quantify if possible: "reduced latency by 40%", "shipped 2 weeks early". Always say what you learned.
